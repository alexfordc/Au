Remarks
To activate a mouse move trigger, the user quickly moves the mouse pointer to the specified direction and back.
The screen is divided into 3 parts: 1 - center 50%; 2 - left or top 25%; 3 - right or bottom 25%. Constants like UpDownInCenter50 specify a direction and screen part; the trigger works only in that screen part. Constants like UpDown specify just a direction; the trigger works in whole screen.
