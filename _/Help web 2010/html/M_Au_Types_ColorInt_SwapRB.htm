ColorIntSwapRB Method

Au Class Library
Converts color from ARGB (0xAARRGGBB) to ABGR (0xAABBGGRR) or vice versa (swaps the red and blue bytes).
ARGB is used in .NET, GDI+ and HTML/CSS.
ABGR is used by most Windows native API.
