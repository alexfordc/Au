SegParser Structure

See Also

Splits a string or StringSegment into StringSegments. Used with foreach. Also used internally by some functions of this library, for example Split_(string,string,SegFlags) and SplitLines_. Normally you don't create Segmenter instances explicitly; instead use Segments_(string,string,SegFlags) or Split with foreach.
